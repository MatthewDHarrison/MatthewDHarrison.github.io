ABOUT TUNEUP

TuneUp is a project dedicated to finding old, unused musical instruments, giving them new life, and putting them in the hands of kids who are just starting out.

Playing an instrument can be a life-changing experience, but unfortunately, the cost of equipment can present a barrier to entry. We aim to overcome that.
